Impossible List

This page was inspired by Thomas Frank Impossible List, itself inspired by Joel Runyon.

Over the years I have been writing multiple, duplicate, ever-evolving lists of things I wanted to do or visit, sadly this did not result into any meaningful change. So with this page, I have several goals:

  • Challenge myself.
  • simplify other pages which was growing out of control with the listing of various aspirations.
  • enable better tracking and force myself to take action on my list of goals.
  • compile old multiple lists on one page.

This impossible list, is not a bucket list, even if at times it can feels like it, it is a way to challenge myself, to push me outside my comfort-zone.

This list is and will be under constant construction and revision (thanks git).
